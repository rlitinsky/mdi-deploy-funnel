Definitions & caveats

  • Licensed universe: any commercial active tenant with a paid MDI seat (AATPPaidAvailableUnits > 0) — M365/A5/EMS E5 etc. This is the full entitlement universe, not the targeted install base, which is why the deployed share is ~20%.
  • Deployed: tenant has any Monthly Protected Users (TotalActiveProtectedUsers > 0) in the rolling 7-day WorkspaceBusinessData feed. Not-deployed includes both "no telemetry row" and "row with MPU = 0".
  • MDE on DC: at least one domain controller (ProductType == 'DomainController') heartbeating to MDE within the last 15 days. A tenant whose DCs stopped heartbeating >15d ago reads as "No MDE".
  • Unmapped: 13,408 not-deployed tenants (10.6%) could not be resolved from AAD tenant ID to an MDE OrgId, so their MDE-on-DC status is unknown — reported as a separate bucket, not folded into "No MDE".
  • Gov excluded: filtered to TenantCloudType == 'Other' (commercial) plus SKU regex exclusion of DOD/GCC.
  • Numbers reconcile exactly: 32,682 + 126,917 = 159,599; 12,487 + 101,022 + 13,408 = 126,917.
