Definitions & caveats

  • Licensed universe: commercial active tenants with both a paid MDI seat (AATPPaidAvailableUnits > 0) and a paid MDE seat (MDATPPaidAvailableUnits > 0). This is the dual-entitlement universe — tenants that could deploy MDI and already own MDE.
  • Deployed: tenant has any Monthly Protected Users (TotalActiveProtectedUsers > 0) in the rolling 7-day WorkspaceBusinessData feed. Not-deployed includes both "no telemetry row" and "row with MPU = 0".
  • MDE on DC: at least one domain controller (ProductType == 'DomainController') heartbeating to MDE within the last 15 days. A tenant whose DCs stopped heartbeating >15d ago reads as "No MDE".
  • Unmapped: 10,893 not-MDI-deployed tenants (9.58%) could not be resolved from AAD tenant ID to an MDE OrgId, so their MDE-on-DC status is unknown — reported as a separate bucket, not folded into "No MDE".
  • Gov excluded: filtered to TenantCloudType == 'Other' (commercial) plus SKU regex exclusion of DOD/GCC.
  • Numbers reconcile exactly: 31,220 + 113,761 = 144,981; 12,024 + 90,844 + 10,893 = 113,761.
